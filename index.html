Our feature

Satellites Constellation

We are deploying a constellation of more than ten satellites in low Earth orbit to enhance data volume and quality, providing more accurate predictive models and richer scientific data.

Real-time Early Warnings

Through preliminary onboard computations and ground-based modeling, the monitored data can be processed to provide real-time early warnings, allowing ground-based systems more time to respond and minimize potential damage.

Satellite Insurance

Insurance companies and satellite manufacturers can also utilize the data to assess the space weather conditions of mission locations, serving as a basis for satellite insurance underwriting.

Sustainability

The related data can be provided to academic institutions for research purposes or serve as a reference for satellite mission development, enhancing our understanding of space weather and improving the planning timeline of space missions.
